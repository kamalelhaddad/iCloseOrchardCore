Why Choose iClose

It is the first A.I. conveyancing software platform that was designed by real estate lawyers & clerks for real estate lawyers & clerks. It includes all of the essential needs to run a real estate firm into one cohesive platform.

  • iClose is a Web-Based Application!

    We’re a web-based application, meaning all you need to use iClose is an internet connection. You do not need servers and other expensive IT infrastructure

  • It’s Easy to Transition to iClose

  • iClose is a Canadian company

    We are proudly a Canadian company. All data is stored in Canada at three (3) different tier 3 data centers throughout Canada.

  • Up to 500% cheaper than the competition

    iClose is very competitively priced. iClose is $25 plus HST, up to 500% less than the competition while providing more functionalities and a superior user experience
